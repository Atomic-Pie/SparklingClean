Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions Answered

How long does an oven clean take?

It depends on the complexity, size and state of your appliances. It can be 90 minutes to 6 hours, but we'll let you know the estimated time when we quote.

What should I send for a quote?

Just send us photos of what needs cleaning (oven, carpets, sofa, etc.) and your location. We'll check if we cover your area and provide a quote quickly.

Do I need to do anything before you arrive?

No preparation needed! Just make sure the oven is switched off and cool. We bring all equipment and products needed.

Are the fumes safe? What about pets?

Yes! We use non caustic, eco friendly products that are safe for your family and pets. No harsh chemical smell or dangerous fumes.

Do you need access to water and electricity?

Yes, we'll need access to a power socket and water supply (kitchen tap is perfect). Everything else is provided by us.

How far do you travel from Carlow?

We're based in Carlow and regularly serve surrounding areas. Send us your location and we'll confirm if we can reach you.
